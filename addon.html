Known FAQ

How do I install the addon? +
Version Compatibility The addon won't always be compatible with the latest version of ClickCrystals. If it crashes, verify your game version.
  1. Ensure you have a compatible version of ClickCrystals installed.
  2. Download the No-oNe-Addon .jar file from the Releases tab.
  3. Place the file into the mods/ folder of your Minecraft instance.
  4. Launch the game. Addon modules will automatically appear in the ClickCrystals GUI under the Addon category.
Why aren't some modules working immediately? +
Restart Required Some modules make deep changes to the UI or game interactions.
Specifically, modules like Mod Menu Disabler require a full game restart to take effect after you toggle them.
How do I report a bug or crash? +
Join our Discord server and head to the bugs channel.

Please include:
  • Your crash log (found in .minecraft/crash-reports/)
  • Your latest log (found in .minecraft/logs/latest.log)
  • A short description of what you were doing when the issue occurred.
Bug reports without logs will be ignored as they cannot be effectively debugged.
Will this get me banned on multiplayer servers? +
Anti-cheats do not detect it in ~99% of cases, but no cheat client or addon can guarantee 100% safety.

Always use it on servers where you understand the rules and risks. We are not responsible for any account bans.
